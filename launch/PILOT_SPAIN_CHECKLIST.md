# Pilot Spain Checklist

## Before pilot

- Prepare demo environment.
- Prepare Spanish landing page.
- Prepare WhatsApp sales script.
- Prepare onboarding form.
- Prepare demo data.
- Prepare pricing offer.

## Pilot targets

First cities:

- Marbella
- Málaga
- Alicante
- Valencia

Target customers:

- property managers with 10–100 units;
- agencies;
- owners with 3–20 properties.

## Pilot offer

- 30 days free.
- Setup assistance.
- Up to 10 properties imported.
- Guest guide setup.
- Cleaning task automation.

## Questions to ask customers

1. How do you currently manage cleaning?
2. How many WhatsApp groups do you use?
3. How do you collect photo reports?
4. How do guests receive instructions?
5. What is your biggest daily problem?
6. Would you pay €49–149/month for this?

## Pilot success

- 3 companies onboarded.
- 30+ properties.
- 100+ tasks.
- 2 paying customers.
